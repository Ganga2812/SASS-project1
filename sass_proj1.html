How to play?

There are a series of cards displayed above. The aim of the game is to memorize which pictures are behind which cards, however you only get a limited number of viewings. Click and hold the show button at the top of the screen to play an animation which will temporarily show you all the pictures behind the cards. Based on the difficulty you want to play, you will click the button a certain number of times giving you the chance to memorize them. After you think you have it, click the answers card to see the correct answers. You can then see how great your memorization skills really are by checking where you rank.

Difficulty:
Easy - 5 shows
Medium - 3 shows
Hard - 1 show
